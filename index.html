Ukraine, February 2020

I've always loved history, and i've been fascinated by the the story of the Chernobyl Nuclear Disaster in the former USSR since watching a documentary on building the sarcophagus over the doomed reactor number 4 to prevent a further radiation disaster over Europe. In February I booked flights and an apartment in the beautiful city of Kyiv (very inexpensively), and pursuaded two friends to join me for the trip.

A visit to the exclusion zone is quite restricted and only certain tour operators are permitted to take guests on 'educational visits', we used a company called Chernoby Tour® which i'd highly recommend. This was a one day tour starting early with a 1.5 hour drive to the exclusion zone.

Mallorca, October 2019

5 days of cycling around the island of Mallorca in October, my first 5 days of back 2 back riding taking in some of the greatest climbs Mallorca has to offer.

Japan, September 2019

In 2019, myself and 5 others flew into Tokyo for a the 2019 Rugby World Cup coupled with travelling the incredibe country of Japan. Tokyo > Nagoya > City of Toyota > Matsumuto > Kamikochi (Japan Alps) > Matsumuto > Kanazawa > Kyoto > Osaka > Kobe > Hiroshima > Itsukushima (Miyajima) > Tokyo.
